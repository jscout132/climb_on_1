Become A Member

Membership to Climb On comes with some amazing perks:

  • 10% off at our stores
  • Guest passes every month to bring a friend
  • Free gear rental every month in case you forget something
  • Access to classes and group meet ups
  • Quarterly raffles to win prizes
  • And more! We're always looking for ways to appreciate our amazing member family

How to become a member:

Step 1: Stop by the gym

We have a bit of papework to go over with you

Step 2: Set up your payment method

We'll bill you monthly and get autopayments set up so you don't have to worry about remembering to pay

Step 3: Make sure you're trained

We'll go over a few quick safety reminders and details to make sure everyone stays safe while climbing

Still have questions? Check our FAQ page to see what's up
