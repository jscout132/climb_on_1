First Visit

If it's your first time climbing with us, we want to make sure you have the most rewarding experience possible. When you come in, please let a staff member know it's your first visit and we'll get you set up. We welcome first-time visitors to Climb On with a discounted day pass rate. For participants ages 12+, climbing shoes and a harness included in the price. For kids 11 and under, a harness is included.

For everyone's safety, we'll ask you to watch a quick introduction to climbing video. This will cover suggested ways of moving throughout the gym, details of our route grades, and a few safety tips. This will help ensure that everyone has a basic understanding of these subjects and expectations. Even if you've climbed at other gyms before, we'll still ask you to watch. It's only a few minutes.

Additionally, we'll ask you to review and sign safety waivers. For more informations about what is covered in these waivers, please ask a staff member.
