Climb On Classes

Take your skills to the next level with some of our classes. Whether you're looking to reach your fitness goals and get in shape, start your yoga journey, or join a group for some outdoor climbing, we have a class for you.

The next time you're in the gym, check out the bulletin board to see our most up to date class schedules. Some classes are included in a gym membership, while others come at an additional cost. Gym staff can also provide additional information on current and upcoming classes!
